Portfolio

Whatever business you are in, a web site is one of the most important investments you can make. Some people compare it to a business card, but of course it’s much more important. In today’s online-centered world, it is more important than your front-office or store. If your web site looks professional, your potential clients will know you are reliable and wouldn’t compromise with quality. If potential clients visit your web site and it looks amateur and home-built that’s how they’ll perceive you. Even if you’re primarily brick and mortar, having a solid web site can mean extra business. Local clientele often perform seaches online and finding your web site, will encourage them to walk into your store. If they find your competitors and they look more reputable or solid than you (or if they have a web site and you don’t), you’ll lose business, just because of your web site. And did you ever think about getting orders from another corner of the world? Launching a fantastic web site means you’ll instantly become a global business, allowing you to expand your clientele to a much larger audience.

Performance

Page loading time is obviously an important part of any website’s user experience. According to surveys done by Brand Perfect, Akamai and Dynatrace nearly half of web users expect a site to load in 2 seconds or less, and they tend to abandon a site that isn’t loaded within 3 seconds. 79% of web shoppers who have trouble with web site performance say they won’t return to the site to buy again, and around 44% of them would tell a friend if they had a poor experience shopping online. Two thirds (67%) of UK shoppers and more than half (51%) of those in the US said that site slowness is the top reason they’d abandon a purchase. Lightning-fast page load speed amplifies visitor engagement, retention, and boosts sales. Instantaneous website response leads to maximum conversion rates, and every 1 second delay in page load decreases customer satisfaction by 16 percent. That’s why speed is a main features of the sites I create.This is achieved by Clear code and structure, caching and minification, proper use of a Content Delivery Network and leveraging modern web technologies for visualization like lazy-loading , progressive jpeg-s and data URI schemes. That’s how I achieved rank 100 on Google PageSpeed Insights for WebOlution.96.lt.

SEO

If people cannot find your web site in search, then it is like the web site doesn’t exist. That is why a web site needs to be SEO-optimized. Organic search still drives the most traffic and conversions to websites, and is still the fastest growing source of traffic for most sites. Consumers are now using search engines to find almost anything from products, services, etc. And this will continue to grow. According to researches the top spot on Google SERPs gets 36.4% of all clicks, the #2 spot gets 12.5%, #3 takes 9.5%, and from there it declines quickly. Being at the top of the heap wins you ‘front of mind’ recall and brand awareness which secures more sales. In order to get sites on first places on Google I’m following Google and Moz guidelines. In order to be searchable a website needs to have a clear hierarchy and structure. Every page should be reachable, the titles and alt tags should be accurately selected. The sitemap and robots.txt file need to be correctly configured. If a site is well-structured investment in SEO will be lower and more effective.
